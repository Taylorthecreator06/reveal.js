Project Overview

What is the project, and what does it do?

Mindustry is an open source tower defense and strategy game made with Java.

Who is it for?

Mindustry is for anyone who enjoys casual gaming, or just looking to have some fun!

Non-Code Contribution Opportunities

Open Areas:

📚 Documentation   🌐 Translation   🎨 Sprite Design   🐞 Bug Reports

Examples:

  • In-game hint PR
  • Translation PR
  • Sprite Design Guide
  • Bug Report Issue

Why Non-Code Contributions are Valuble

  • 📚 Documentation improves usabilibility
  • 🌐 Translation expands accessibility to others
  • 🎨 Sprites enhance visuals and user experience
  • 🐞 Bug reports help identify and fix issues to imrpove functionality

Contribution Guidelines

Where are the Guidelines Found?

Under the CONTRIBUTING.md

Is the onboarding process beginner-friendly?

Yes! There are so many options available to contribute to the game. The community on discord aencourages non programmers to get involved!
